About me

I am a freelance writer for various online and print publications local to Northern Virginia. As a columnist at Fairfax County Times and contributing writer for Rappahannock Media, I am a features and news journalist specializing in local businesses, health/science, education, and arts/entertainment.
I practiced concise writing while pursuing a Bachelor of Science in biology from George Mason University, which (ironically) only strengthened my ability to dress my writing up.
